French Toast

Why?

French toast is a hearty and delicious meal perfect for a late Sunday breakfast

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 banana, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • butter, for frying
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • strawberries, for serving

Method

  1. Combine eggs, milk and mashed banana in a flat-bottommed bowl
  2. Combine sugar and ground cinnamon in small bowl
  3. Heat frying pan with a small amount of butter
  4. Once hot, dip one slice of bread into mixture. Ensure you coat both sides
  5. Fry on a medium heat until golden brown and crispy
  6. Repeat until mixture is done
  7. Serve with cut strawberries and cinnamon sugar to taste
